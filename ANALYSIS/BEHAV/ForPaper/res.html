Mediation analysis

Mediation analysis was performed based on the counter-factual framework and the interventional effect (Vansteelandt and Daniel, 2017; Chan and Leung, 2020). The analysis was conducted in R using the intmed package (Chan and Leung, 2020) with 1000 simulations.
Results from the mediation analysis indicated that
  • the indirect effect through HPP was statistically significant, p = 0.018; -16.512% of the total effect was mediated through HPP.
  • the direct effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
  • the total effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
